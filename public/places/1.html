Campus Landmarks

Joe and Rika Mansueto Library

The Mansueto Library can hold the equivalent of 3.5 million volumes through its automated storage and retrieval system. Mansueto's design reflects Regenstein Library next door, as well as Mitchell Tower beyond. Though Mansueto's glass dome structure creates a sense of permeability between the indoors and the outdoors, it is closely temperature controlled, staying cool in the hot summers and warm in the cold winters.
